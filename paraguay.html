National Food-Sopa Paraguaya

Sopa Paraguaya is considered the national dish of Paraguay due to a popular legend about its accidental creation during the presidency of Carlos Antonio LÃ³pez. The story claims a chef, attempting to make corn soup, mistakenly added too much cornmeal, resulting in a solid, baked dish instead. The president enjoyed the creation so much that he named it "Sopa Paraguaya" (Paraguayan soup), despite it being a dense cornbread.
