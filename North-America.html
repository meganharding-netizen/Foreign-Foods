About

North American food is diverse, reflecting influences from Indigenous, European, African, and Asian cuisines, with common characteristics including bold flavors, grilling, and the use of ingredients like corn, beans, and potatoes. Key aspects include large portions, comfort foods like fried chicken and cheeseburgers, and a wide range of regional dishes from Tex-Mex to Canadian poutine.
