National Food-Hamburger

The hamburger was popularized and evolved into a unique sandwich in the United States, especially through its mass production and the rise of fast food chains. While its roots are in German-style ground beef, it gained national recognition at the 1904 St. Louis World's Fair and was further cemented as an American staple by 20th-century innovations like the hamburger bun and drive-ins.
