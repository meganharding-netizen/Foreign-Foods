About

Asian food is a diverse culinary tradition characterized by its complex and balanced flavors, often combining sweet, sour, salty, spicy, and umami notes. Key elements include the extensive use of fresh, seasonal ingredients, aromatic herbs and spices like ginger, garlic, and lemongrass, and a balance of protein, vegetables, and grains, such as rice and noodles. Common cooking methods include stir-frying, steaming, and boiling, which preserve the fresh ingredients.
