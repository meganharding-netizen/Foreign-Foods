Bahrain

National Food-Machboos

Machboos is a flavorful and aromatic rice and meat dish that embodies the country's rich cultural heritage and its history as a hub for the spice trade. The dish is a staple in Bahraini households and a symbol of Arabian flavors, combining a unique blend of spices like baharat and dried limes with tender meat and rice.
