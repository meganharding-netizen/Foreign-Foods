About

European food is a diverse and rich culinary tradition encompassing distinct national cuisines that often feature fresh, seasonal ingredients. Common elements include hearty stews, meats, and starches like potatoes and bread, with a significant variety of cheeses and dairy products. While Southern European cuisines might emphasize olive oil and herbs, Northern European styles can be more robust, with dishes like smoked fish and rich stews, though both regions often feature a balance of tradition and modern creativity.
