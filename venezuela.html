Venezuela

National Food-Pabellón Criollo

Pabellón criollo, is considered its national food because it symbolizes the country's multicultural history and culinary heritage. The dish combines ingredients from the three main cultural influences that shaped Venezuela: European, African, and Indigenous. Each component of pabellón criollo represents a different group, making it a flavorful representation of the nation's diverse people and history.
