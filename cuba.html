Cuba

National Food-Ropa Vieja

Ropa Vieja is a beloved staple that represents Cuban culture's resilience, history, and culinary ingenuity, despite its origins in Spain. The dish's name, which means "old clothes," comes from a legend of a poor man cooking his tattered clothing to feed his family, turning it into a miracle stew, which symbolizes resourcefulness during hard times.
